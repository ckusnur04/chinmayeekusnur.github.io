About the internship
This January, I started working at Nokia as an Interface UX Designer. I am in the organization's Network Infrastructure (NI) team and directly work with UX Designers and Researchers.
My primary responsibilities include-
1. Transferring Nokia's design libraries from Skektch to Figma and building components from scratch.
2. Designing slides for NI's UX Summit held in February 2023.
3. Helping the UX research team conduct user workshops for network product sales.
4. Building interaction specifications that outline the UX flow and applying sound user-centred design principles to one of their products for developers.
