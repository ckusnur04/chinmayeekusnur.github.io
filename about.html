Since I was a kid, I've always designed with empathy, believing I have a natural knack for understanding what people want, whether they're users or businesses. This understanding is key for any designer. I've been passionate about the connection between data and design. I use design thinking and user research to solve problems, always making sure to prioritize the end goal.
Outside of design, I'm involved in projects that support sustainability and community empowerment. As a runner-up for the UN Women Award, I've been part of initiatives like distributing Khadi face masks during the COVID pandemic.
I'm on a journey to make a positive impact through design, and I'm eager to connect with others who share this passion. Whether it's talking about design, sustainability, or sharing travel stories, I'm all ears. Feel free to reach out to me!
