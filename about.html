About me

Hello! I'm a design student in my final semester at Simon Fraser University, originally from vibrant Mumbai 🌆. My journey involves exploring the dynamic relationship between data and design, applying design thinking to solve complex problems 💡📊🎨. I prioritize ethics and principles, aiming to create user-centric solutions that are ethical, inclusive, and sustainable.

I'm honored to have received runner-up recognition for the UN Women Award, distributing Khadi face masks during COVID-19 to promote sustainability and empower women artisans. Beyond design, I'm a food enthusiast and avid traveler 🍔✈️, often drawing inspiration from different cultures for my work.

I'm enthusiastic about collaborating with like-minded professionals and organizations to drive positive change through data-driven design. Let's connect and chat about design opportunities, sustainability, or share travel stories over coffee ☕. Feel free to reach out to me at ckusnur@sfu.ca. Excited to hear from you!

Thank you for stopping by!

I am open to design opportunities!
