About me

Hello! I'm a fourth-year design student at Simon Fraser University, originally from the vibrant city of Mumbai. My journey in design has been amazing, exploring the dynamic relationship between data and design. Through co-op internships, I've applied design thinking to solve complex problems.

Ethics and principles guide my approach to design, focusing on creating user-centric solutions that are ethical, inclusive, and sustainable. I'm proud of my runner-up recognition for the UN Women Award, where I distributed Khadi face masks during COVID-19, promoting sustainability and empowering women artisans.

Outside design, I love exploring food and traveling to discover new cultures and perspectives, which often inspires my work. I'm always open to collaborating with like-minded professionals and organizations to drive positive change through data-driven design.

Let's connect and discuss design opportunities, sustainability, or swap travel stories over coffee. Looking forward to hearing from you! Please email me at ckusnur@sfu.ca to get in touch with me.
